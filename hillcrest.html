The Story

WHO WE
ARE

Hillcrest is Mateo and Tábo — two YNs from the west side who found music music as an outlet for the signals they received and felt from their surrounds. They built from the same blocks we grew up on: soulful, bright, and unapologetically West Coast, with room room to explore all the soudns that drive their lives

We make music for the ones who feel deeply, think critically, and speak proundly. Music is the language — and together, they build commuinty with family, friends, and neighbors.
